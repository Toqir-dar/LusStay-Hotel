Our Story

Founded in 2010, LuxStay Hotel has been a cornerstone of luxury hospitality for over 15 years. We began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience unparalleled comfort, personalized service, and exquisite amenities.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of the premier luxury accommodations in the region, having welcomed guests from over 100 countries and received numerous industry accolades for our commitment to excellence.
